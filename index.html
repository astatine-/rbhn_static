About Our Club

Rotary Bengaluru Heritage North is part of the global Rotary network, dedicated to creating positive change in our community and beyond.

Is it the TRUTH?

We strive for honesty and transparency in all our actions and communications.

Is it FAIR to all concerned?

We ensure equity and justice in our decisions and service projects.

Will it build GOODWILL?

We foster better friendships and stronger community bonds through our work.

Will it be BENEFICIAL?

We ensure our actions benefit all concerned and create lasting positive impact.

Rotary's Areas of Focus

We address the world's most pressing challenges through our seven areas of focus

Basic Education & Literacy

Supporting education initiatives and literacy programs to empower communities.

Water, Sanitation & Hygiene

Providing access to clean water and sanitation facilities for healthy communities.

Disease Prevention & Treatment

Fighting diseases and improving healthcare access in underserved areas.

Environment

Protecting our planet through sustainable environmental initiatives.

Peace & Conflict Prevention

Building peace and preventing conflicts through understanding and cooperation.

Economic Development

Creating opportunities for economic growth and community development.
